Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Budget Buddy! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our app. By using Budget Buddy, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Budget Buddy collects the following types of information:

  • Personal Information:
    • Income and Expenses: When you add your income and expenses to the app, this information is stored locally on your device.
    • Categories: The categories associated with your income and expenses are also stored locally.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for the purpose of helping you manage your finances. We do not share, sell, or otherwise disclose your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage

All data entered into Budget Buddy is stored locally on your device using Hive storage. This means your data is not transmitted to or stored on any external servers or cloud services.

Data Deletion

When you choose to delete your data from Budget Buddy, it is completely removed from the Hive storage on your device. This action is irreversible, and the data cannot be recovered once deleted.

Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please be aware that no security measure is perfect, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app, and you are encouraged to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
